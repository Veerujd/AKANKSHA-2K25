24-Hour Hackathon

Build innovative solutions to real-world problems in this round-the-clock coding marathon.

Participants will have 24 hours to design, develop, and present a working prototype of their solution. The theme will be announced at the start of the event.

Rules & Guidelines

Teams must consist of 2-4 members. All members must be currently enrolled students with valid ID cards.

All code must be written during the hackathon. You can use any programming language, framework, or library. Pre-built templates are allowed but must be declared.

Projects must be submitted via GitHub repository link along with a brief presentation (max 5 slides) explaining the problem and solution.
Event Details
Date: Oct 15, 2025
Time: 9:00 AM - 9:00 AM
Venue: Main Auditorium
Team Size: 2-4 Members
Fee: ₹500 per team

Robotics Workshop & Competition

Design, build and program robots to complete challenging tasks in this exciting competition.

Participants will learn the basics of robotics in a workshop session and then compete in a robot race and obstacle course challenge.

Rules & Guidelines

Robots must fit within a 25cm x 25cm x 25cm dimension. Maximum weight limit is 3kg. Power source must be on-board.

The competition consists of three rounds: Line following, Obstacle avoidance, and Sumo wrestling. Teams must qualify each round to advance.
Event Details
Date: Oct 16, 2025
Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Venue: Robotics Lab
Team Size: 2-3 Members
Fee: ₹300 per team
Register Now

Code Sprint Challenge

Test your coding skills in this intense algorithmic programming competition.

Participants will solve a series of challenging programming problems within a limited time frame. Languages allowed: C, C++, Java, Python.

Rules & Guidelines

The competition will consist of 5 problems of varying difficulty. Points are awarded based on successful solutions and time taken.

This is an individual competition. No team participation allowed. Internet access will be restricted during the competition.
Event Details
Date: Oct 17, 2025
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Venue: Computer Lab 3
Participation: Individual
Fee: ₹100 per person
