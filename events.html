About Our College

B.S. Channabasappa First Grade College

Established with a vision to provide quality education and foster holistic development, B.S. Channabasappa First Grade College has been a beacon of knowledge since its inception.

Affiliated with the renowned University of Mysore, our institution has consistently produced outstanding alumni who have excelled in diverse fields including technology, business, arts, and public service.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and ethical values to become future leaders and responsible global citizens.

About AAKANKSHA

AAKANKSHA, our annual techno-cultural fest, began in 2007-08 with a vision to create a platform for students to showcase their talents beyond academics. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated college events in the region.

This year, as we celebrate our 20th anniversary, AAKANKSHA 2K25 promises to be bigger and better with the theme "Empire of Innovation". The fest aims to foster innovative thinking and develop managerial and technical skills among participants.

Last year, we hosted over 2,000 participants from 50+ colleges across the state. This year, we're expanding further with more events, workshops, and opportunities for students to learn, compete, and grow.

Event Details

Recruitoria Kingdom

Where talent meets opportunity

Recruitoria Kingdom is a platform for students to showcase their skills to industry leaders and recruiters. This event simulates real-world recruitment processes and provides valuable feedback from professionals.

Rules

Open to all college students. Individual participation only. Valid college ID required.

The event consists of three rounds: Resume screening, group discussion, and personal interview.

Participants will be evaluated on communication skills, subject knowledge, problem-solving ability, and overall presentation.

General Rules & Regulations

  • All participants must carry their college ID cards and the fest registration receipt.
  • Participants should report at the venue 30 minutes prior to the event timing.
  • The decision of judges and event organizers will be final and binding.
  • Any form of indiscipline or misbehavior will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Teams must consist of members from the same institution (except for open events).
  • The organizers reserve the right to change the rules or cancel any event if necessary.
  • Electronic gadgets (calculators, smart watches, etc.) may be restricted in certain events.
  • Outside help or references are not allowed unless explicitly permitted.
