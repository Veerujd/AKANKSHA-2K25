About AAKANKSHA 2K25

The Premier Management & Tech Fest

AAKANKSHA 2K25 is a state-level management and tech fest that brings together the brightest minds to compete, innovate, and celebrate knowledge.

Organized by B.S. Channabasappa First Grade College, this event features a diverse range of competitions, workshops, and exhibitions that challenge participants to push their boundaries and showcase their talents.
