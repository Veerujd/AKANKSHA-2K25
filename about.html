About Sri Vinayaka Educational Trust

Our Legacy of Excellence

Sri Vinayaka Educational Trust was established with a vision to provide quality education and foster innovation in the region.

The trust has been instrumental in establishing educational institutions that cater to diverse fields of study, with B.S. Channabasappa First Grade College being one of its flagship institutions.

With a commitment to holistic education and community development, the trust continues to set benchmarks in the education sector.

College History

Journey of excellence in education

Our Journey

B.S. Channabasappa First Grade College, located in S.S. Layout A Block, Davanagere, has been a beacon of quality education in the region. The college is named after the esteemed philanthropist and educationist, Sri B.S. Channabasappa.

Established under the auspices of Sri Vinayaka Education Trust (R), the college has consistently provided excellent education in various disciplines, focusing on both academic excellence and character building.

Over the years, the college has grown significantly, adding new courses and facilities to meet the evolving educational needs of students. Our alumni have excelled in various fields, making significant contributions to society.
